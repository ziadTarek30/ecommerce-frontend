FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find answers to common questions about orders, shipping, returns, and more.

Ordering & Payment

How do I place an order? ▼
Browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to provide shipping information and payment details. Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive an email with your order number and tracking information.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Shop Pay. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
Can I modify or cancel my order? ▼
You can edit or cancel pending orders from your account page. Once an order has been shipped, you won't be able to modify it, but you can initiate a return once you receive the items. Contact us immediately if you need help with an order.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take? ▼
Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days and is free on all orders. Express shipping (2-3 business days) is available for $9.99. Overnight shipping is available for $24.99. Processing typically takes 1-2 business days before your order ships.
Do you ship internationally? ▼
Yes! We ship to over 30 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. Customs fees and import taxes may apply and are the responsibility of the customer.
How can I track my order? ▼
Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order from your account page. Tracking information is typically available within 24 hours of shipment.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy? ▼
We offer free returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached. Sale items and accessories are final sale. Start a return from your account page or contact our support team.
How do I exchange an item? ▼
To exchange an item for a different size or color, initiate a return and place a new order for the item you want. This ensures you get the new item as quickly as possible. You'll receive a full refund once we receive your return.
When will I receive my refund? ▼
Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days of receiving your return. The refund will be credited to your original payment method. Please allow 5-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your bank or credit card company.

Products & Sizing

How do I find my size? ▼
Each product page includes a size guide with measurements. We recommend measuring yourself and comparing to our size chart for the best fit. Our clothing fits true to size, but if you're between sizes, we suggest sizing up for a more relaxed fit.
Are your products sustainable? ▼
Yes! We're committed to sustainability. Many of our products are made from organic cotton, recycled materials, and eco-friendly fabrics. We work with ethical manufacturers and continuously improve our environmental practices.
How do I care for my items? ▼
Care instructions are included on the label of each item. Generally, we recommend machine washing in cold water with like colors and tumble drying on low. Avoid bleach and harsh detergents to maintain the quality and color of your garments.

Account & Other

Do I need an account to place an order? ▼
No, you can checkout as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to track orders, save addresses, view order history, and receive exclusive offers. It only takes a minute to sign up!
How do I contact customer support? ▼
You can reach us via email at support@urbanthreads.com, call us at +1 (555) 123-4567 (Mon-Fri, 9AM-6PM EST), or use the live chat on our website (available 24/7). Visit our Contact page for more options.
Do you offer student or military discounts? ▼
Yes! We offer a 15% discount for students and military personnel. Verify your status through our verification partner to receive your discount code. The discount applies to full-price items only.
