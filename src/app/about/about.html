Our Story

Urban Threads started in 2020 with a simple idea: streetwear should be accessible, authentic, and bold. Founded by a group of designers who grew up in the city, we saw a gap between high-fashion streetwear and fast fashion—quality pieces that expressed individuality without breaking the bank.

What began as a small collection of graphic tees has evolved into a full lifestyle brand. We work directly with emerging artists, designers, and creators who share our vision of making fashion that matters. Every piece tells a story. Every design has purpose.

Today, Urban Threads is worn by creatives, entrepreneurs, and dreamers across the globe. We're not just selling clothes—we're building a community of people who refuse to blend in.
